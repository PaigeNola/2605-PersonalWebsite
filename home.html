Telling stories with authenticity and connecting with other humans are the cornerstones of a life well-lived.

Hailing from one the largest Mexican families in the city, Omaha native Paige Nola is a versatile bassist and actor who has loved storytelling and music from a young age. Trained at the New York Conservatory of Dramatic Arts, she is highly active in the local film and theater scene, acting, and production credits including Inner Worlds, Who’s Watching, and The Dinner Detective.

She plays genres ranging from jazz to rock to salsa and recently performed for the Nebraska Theater Academy Showcase. Paige is entering her sixth year directing Millard North High School’s show choir band, Insanity, providing students with realistic gigging experience.

Paige enjoys crocheting, chocolate, training her dog, and laughing with friends.
